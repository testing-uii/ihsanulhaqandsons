Our Story

Our journey began in 1990 with a small neighborhood shop founded by our father. What started as a humble store with limited items has grown over the years into a trusted and well-known supermarket in the community.

From day one, our goal has always been simple — to provide quality products, fair prices, and friendly service to our customers. With hard work, dedication, and the trust of our loyal customers, we have expanded step by step while staying true to our values.

Today, we are proud to continue our father’s legacy by serving the community with a wider range of products, improved facilities, and the same personal touch that made us who we are.

We believe a supermarket is not just a place to shop, but a place that connects people, families, and everyday needs.

Thank you for being a part of our journey.

— Ihsan Ul Haq & Sons Family
